# Image Assets Guide

## Directory Structure

```
public/images/
├── branding/
│   ├── logo.png          - Main Achievers Club logo (recommended: 200x60px)
│   ├── logo-light.png    - Light version for dark backgrounds
│   └── icon.png          - Favicon/app icon (recommended: 512x512px)
├── team/
│   ├── leader-1.jpg      - Team leader photo 1
│   ├── leader-2.jpg      - Team leader photo 2
│   ├── leader-3.jpg      - Team leader photo 3
│   └── team-group.jpg    - Group photo of the team
├── office/
│   ├── guntur-office.jpg     - Guntur office photo
│   ├── hyderabad-office.jpg  - Hyderabad office photo
│   └── mumbai-office.jpg     - Mumbai office photo
└── hero/
    └── hero-bg.jpg       - Hero section background (recommended: 1920x1080px)
```

## Where Images Are Used

### Branding
- **logo.png** - Used in Navbar and Footer
- **icon.png** - Used as favicon in index.html

### Team Photos
- **leader-*.jpg** - Used in About page, Leadership section
- **team-group.jpg** - Used in About page, Community section

### Office Photos
- **office photos** - Used in Contact page for each location

### Hero Background
- **hero-bg.jpg** - Used as background in Home page hero section

## Image Specifications

- **Format:** PNG for logos (transparency), JPG for photos
- **Size:** Optimize for web (< 500KB per image)
- **Dimensions:** 
  - Logo: 200x60px or similar aspect ratio
  - Photos: 1200px width recommended
  - Hero: 1920x1080px or larger

## Adding Your Images

1. Place your images in the appropriate folders
2. Keep the exact filenames as specified above
3. The website will automatically use them

## Placeholder Images

Currently, the website uses placeholder paths. Once you add your actual images, they will replace the placeholders automatically.
